#### Scene-layout:

Sizes

Scene: 64 bytes
Base: 128 bytes
Overlay: 32 bytes/frame

512 bases = 64K bytes
256 scenes = 16384 bytes

That's 5/8ths of a 1mbit eeprom, leaves space for 1536 frames of animation.
